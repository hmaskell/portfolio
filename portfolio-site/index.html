Hello, welcome to my portfolio. I am a design student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Simon Fraser University's School of Interactive Arts and Technology, in Surrey, British Columbia.

I get a little giddy when I can tell something is super well thought through. I strive to achieve that giddyness in the interfaces, products and myriad projects I work on. I am motivated by that moment of clarity after a usability study, putting the final highlights on a painting, and the feeling of finally discovering the cause of a bug in my code.
